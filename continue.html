About Me

Ayasya Kumar Batta is a recent graduate from Delhi Public School Noida with hands-on experience in machine learning research, full-stack development, and robotics engineering. As founder of his school's Robotics Club and captain of the tennis team, he balances high academic achievement with varsity athletics, demonstrating time management, leadership, and resilience. His technical work spans deep learning models trained on multi-million record clinical datasets to accessible navigation systems for visually impaired users, while his athletic pursuits include regional-level competition and mentoring junior players. With a strong track record of delivering measurable results in research and competitions, Ayasya remains committed to building technology that addresses real-world challenges and giving back through community workshops and public health initiatives
